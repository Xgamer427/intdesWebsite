BUTLER

The Butler font is a serif typeface that is inspired the serif fonts everyone knows and loves, mainly the Didot family and Bodoni style, while adding a touch of modernism. Fabian De Smet designed Butler to stand out with its high contrast between thick and thin strokes, sharp direction changes, and its artistic curves. This was to give it a sophisticated appearance, while keeping the typeface approachable. This unique aesthetic makes it suitable for editorial design, branding, refinement, and luxureous elegance.

The versatility Butler offers its users is one of the most defining features of the typeface, coming in multiple weights and styles. Of these styles includes the expected as well as unexpected variations, including an entire stencil form. This flexibility allows designers to be creative with different tones, textures, and variability all within the same project. Maintaining a cohesive look and upholding stunning visual impact, Butler adds a modern twsit to traditional serif design. This gives it the edgy, minimalist feel that works well for bold titles, logos, and high-end fashion magazines.

The details Butler offers ensure it remains readable in both large and small prints. From slightly curved terminals, high contrast strokes, and sophisticated style, it fits perfectly for luxery branding, elegant packaging, and editorial layouts that need that last bit of classic beauty with a modern flair thrown into it.
